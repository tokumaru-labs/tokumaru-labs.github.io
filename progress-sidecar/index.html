Progress Sidecar

Chrome Extension · version 0.1.0 · Tokumaru Labs

See total, completed, remaining, and estimated remaining time at a glance — and update progress right in the side panel.

総量・完了・残り・推定残り時間をひと目で。サイドパネルの一覧のまま進捗を更新できます。

Key features

  • Total, completed, remaining, and estimated remaining time for each task, at a glance.
  • Update progress directly in the list with − / current / total / ＋.
  • Add, edit, and delete tasks, and restore a deleted task from a local snapshot.
  • Export a JSON backup, and import one with a validated preview and a safe Merge.
  • Japanese and English interface, consistent across multiple side panels.

Privacy

  • Tasks, progress, settings, and recovery snapshots are stored only in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • No external transmission, no analytics, no advertising, and no account.
  • Requests only the sidePanel and storage permissions.
  • Full details: Privacy Policy / プライバシーポリシー.

タスク・進捗・設定・復元用スナップショットは端末内の chrome.storage.local にのみ保存。外部送信・分析・広告はありません。
