Progress Sidecar

See total, completed, remaining, and estimated remaining time at a glance.

About

Progress Sidecar is a Chrome side-panel extension for seeing how much work is left on the things you are studying or building. It shows each task's total, completed, remaining, and estimated remaining time, and lets you update progress directly in the list.

Your data is stored only in Chrome on your device. Progress Sidecar does not transmit it to external servers, and there is no analytics or advertising.
