Arcade Counter Timer

Chrome Extension · version 0.1.1 · Tokumaru Labs

A count-up timer and tally counter that stays out of your way — then turns into a small arcade machine for a fraction of a second when you hit a milestone.

邪魔をしないカウントアップタイマーとタリーカウンター。節目にだけ、ほんの一瞬アーケードになります。

Key features

  • Count-up timer in HH:MM:SS that keeps running while the popup is closed and across browser restarts.
  • A tally counter — one press, one count — with its own reset.
  • Local TODAY / WEEK / MONTH / YEAR statistics.
  • Brief milestone feedback: fly text on fast streaks and a CHAIN burst every 10th count.
  • Original sound effects generated with the Web Audio API — no audio files.
  • Every effect (sound, fly text, chain, CRT scanlines) can be switched off. Works entirely offline.

Privacy

  • Works entirely offline with no account and no network communication.
  • Timer state and statistics are kept locally on your device.
  • No analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

完全オフラインで動作し、アカウント不要。タイマーと統計は端末内にのみ保存し、分析・トラッキング・広告はありません。
